Rewrite Policy

As we had previously promised, you will get 10 free rewrites. If you don't like the assignment written by our experts or if you want to make any changes to your assignment, you can request a free rewrite. If you still want any changes in your assignment after 10 free changes, then you have to pay for them. And the cost will be determined by your specifications.

Refund Policy

If you want a full refund or your money back for any reason, then you can request a full refund without any hesitation. But you should keep some terms in mind.

  • There will be no refund if the student got a preview of the assignment.
  • 2.5% of the order value will deduct before refund.
  • You will get your refund within 2-3 working days.

Cancellation Policy

  • If you want to cancel an order, then you have to inform our customer care executives within 24 hours.
  • If you ordered an assignment and then didn't pay for it, your account will be suspended.
  • You have to provide at least two reasons why you are cancelling the order.
