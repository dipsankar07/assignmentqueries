Frequenty Asked Questions

How can I place order?

Fill out the form on the home page. Upload your assignment. Choose a deadline to submit your assignment. Write down your requirements and problems. It's that easy.

How can I get my refund?

Read the refund terms. If you are not violating any of our terms, then contact our customer executive for a full refund.

My payment is done but my order status is stil pending?

If your payment is completed and you got a success message, then don't worry about it; your order status will be updated within 12 hours.

How can I download my assignment?

When our experts have finished working on your assignment, we will send you an email with a link to download your assignment.

How do I make a change request?

If you want any changes in your assignment, then you can call or mail us. And explain the changes that you want in your assignment as provided by us.
